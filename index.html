About Me

  • Associate Professor at the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.
  • Co-Founder of n-hop technologies.

Academic Degrees

  • Bachelor's degree in Electronic Information, Nankai University, 2001.
  • Master's degree in Communication Engineering, Peking University, 2004.
  • PhD in Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, 2008.

Professional Experiences

I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Waterloo from 2008 to 2009 and at the Institute of Network Coding, The Chinese University of Hong Kong from 2010 to 2012. I was with the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences at Tsinghua University from 2012 to 2015.

Though mainly employed in academia, I have actively participated in industrial R&D in recent years. With n-hop technologies, I am developing coding-based network communication technologies.

Research

My research interests include information theory, coding theory, and quantum information.
